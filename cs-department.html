About the Department

The Department of Computer Science at George Mason University is one of the largest and most diverse CS departments in the United States. Our faculty are internationally recognized researchers who push the boundaries of knowledge in areas ranging from artificial intelligence and machine learning to cybersecurity, bioinformatics, and high-performance computing. We pride ourselves on translating research breakthroughs into real-world solutions that benefit society.

Located in the heart of the Northern Virginia technology corridor home to some of the world's leading technology companies and federal agencies GMU CS students enjoy unparalleled access to internship opportunities, industry collaborations, and cutting-edge research facilities. With over 2,000 undergraduate and graduate students, our department fosters an inclusive, innovative community dedicated to academic excellence and professional growth.
